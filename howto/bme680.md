## BME680
* [ESPHOME](https://esphome.io/components/sensor/bme680.html)

## Pins
* D0 -> SDA
* D1 -> SCL
* 3.3V -> 2-6V
* GND -> GND

## ESPHOME
i2c:
  sda: 2
  scl: 15
  scan: True
  id: bus_a

sensor:
  - platform: bme680
    i2c_id: bus_a
    temperature:
      name: "BME680 Temperature"
      oversampling: 16x
    pressure:
      name: "BME680 Pressure"
    humidity:
      name: "BME680 Humidity"
    gas_resistance:
      name: "BME680 Gas Resistance"
    address: 0x77
    update_interval: 60s
